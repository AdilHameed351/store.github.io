About Us

Our Story

We are extremely proud to serve main street neighbourhood. We aim to provide you not only healthy food but we put 100% love and heart into making your food. Our food is made with fresh and top-quality ingredients. We are Asian and middle-eastern cuisine mixed with vegan friendly options and great selection of salads and pizzas. We wish to satisfy our customers with what we offer. We are open to serve you better, and always respect and look forward to your suggestions.
